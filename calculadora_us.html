PRIMO COST


Resultado

*Cousin cost

The prime cost is obtained by adding direct raw material and direct labor and then the result will appear below the word Result and in Production cost.

Production cost


Resultado

*Production cost

The production cost is obtained with the prime cost (which was obtained before), direct manufacturing overhead and direct costs. The result of production cost will also appear under the word Result, in Total cost of operation and Unit cost of production

Indirect costs

Resultado

*Indirect costs

Indirect costs are obtained by adding Indirect Raw Material, Indirect Labor y Indirect Manufacturing General Expenses . The result will appear below the word Result.

Cost of distribution


Resultado

*Distribution costs

The distribution cost is obtained by adding Sales Expenses and Administration Expenses. The result will be displayed under the word Result and will also appear in Total operation cost.

Total cost of operation


Resultado

*Total cost of operation

The total cost of operation is obtained by adding Production cost (already obtained before) and Distribution cost. The result will appear below the word Result.

Unit cost of production

Resultado

*Unit cost of production

The unit cost of production is obtained by dividing the Production Costs y Production Costs. The result will appear below the word Result.
